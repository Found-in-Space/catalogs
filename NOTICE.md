publication repository.

## Repository License Model

- Repository software, scripts, schemas, templates, and documentation owned by
  Found in Space are released under the MIT License unless a file says
  otherwise.
- Catalog publications under `publications/` are versioned academic data works.
  Each publication may carry its own `LICENSE.txt`, `NOTICE.md`, citation
  metadata, and upstream attribution requirements.
- Source catalog data and derived evidence may remain subject to the terms of
  the upstream data providers. A publication license does not remove or replace
  those upstream terms.

## Upstream Data Sources

Found in Space catalog publications may use or derive evidence from external
astronomical data sources. Publications must identify the relevant upstream
sources in their own notice files. Known sources used so far include:

- ESA Gaia Archive / Gaia DR3, including Gaia source data and Gaia-Hipparcos2
  crossmatch tables.
- ESA Hipparcos/Tycho catalogues, including Hipparcos-2 data distributed
  through VizieR/CDS.
- VizieR catalogue access service, CDS, Strasbourg, France.

ESA and cite the relevant Hipparcos/Tycho catalogue publications identified in
