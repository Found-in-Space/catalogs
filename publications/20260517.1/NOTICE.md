# Notice for Publication 20260517.1

Publication: Gaia-HIP review candidate evidence

This publication is a Found-In-Space-authored evidence product built from
objective Gaia DR3 and Hipparcos-2 candidate cohorts. It includes derived
candidate tables, quality flags, crossmatch context, and priority scores for
later review. It does not publish source-row replacement decisions or override
YAML.

Found-In-Space original documentation, scripts, and table arrangement are
licensed under CC BY 4.0 as described in `LICENSE.txt`.

## Upstream Catalogs And Services

This publication depends on identifiers, measurements, and context from:

- Gaia DR3, published by the Gaia Collaboration and accessed through the ESA
  Gaia Archive.
- The Hipparcos and Tycho Catalogues, including the Hipparcos-2 reduction
  available through VizieR.
- VizieR/CDS, for catalog access.
- Found-In-Space publication `20260515.1`, for supplemental Gaia-Hipparcos
  display-map context used as review evidence.

Upstream catalog names, identifiers, and measurements remain subject to their
original terms, citation requirements, and credit requirements. This publication
does not replace the source catalogs or cited scientific literature.

## Scope

This release is limited to systematic Gaia/Hipparcos candidate discovery and
priority ranking for objective bright, nearby, and high-proper-motion cohorts.
Any later replacement values should be recorded as explicit per-target curation
decisions before pipeline YAML override artifacts are generated.

The Sun is intentionally excluded because it remains a pipeline builtin record.
